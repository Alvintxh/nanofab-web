# Microfabrication Processes - BYU Cleanroom

## Overview

The Integrated Microfabrication Lab (IML) at Brigham Young University maintains comprehensive resources for semiconductor fabrication processes. Their website documents "basic steps involved in creating an N-MOS transistor" and provides detailed guides across multiple fabrication domains.

## Main Process Categories

### General Information
- MOSFET fabrication tutorial
- Chemistry recipe calculators for chemical measurements

### Photolithography Resources

**Materials & Equipment:**
- Photoresist manufacturers database
- Adhesion promoters and spin-on materials
- SU-8 recipes and procedures
- AZ 3330 glass adhesion techniques

**Procedures:**
- Interactive lithography tutorials
- Photoresist photolithography step-by-step instructions
- Mask alignment procedures
- Common lithography terminology definitions

**Photomask Production:**
- Cadence design tutorials
- High-resolution printing (50-micron features)
- In-house pattern generator capabilities
- Commercial fabrication vendor links

### Thin Film Deposition & Growth

- Thermal and E-beam evaporation data
- Material property references

**Oxide Growth:**
- Time and thickness calculators
- Temperature and crystal orientation parameters
- Film color prediction tools

- Etchant formulations for 50+ materials

- Substrate cleaning procedures

**Reactive Ion Etching (RIE):**
- Process explanations and recipes
