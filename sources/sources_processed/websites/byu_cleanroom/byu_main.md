# BYU Cleanroom: Research and Teaching at the Nanoscale

## Overview

The Integrated Microfabrication Lab (IML) at Brigham Young University provides cleanroom facilities for research and teaching. Key statistics:

- **Air Cleanliness:** 10 particles or fewer per cubic foot of air (cleanest facility on campus)
- **Available Equipment:** Over 50 machines and instruments accessible to student researchers, including undergraduates
- **Research Impact:** More than 1,000 research publications enabled by cleanroom facilities
